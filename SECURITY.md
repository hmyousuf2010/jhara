# security policy

if you find a security vulnerability, please don't open a public issue. that just gives bad actors a head start.

## reporting

email me directly at **hmyousuf2010@gmail.com**. i'm building this solo, so i might not reply in 5 minutes, but i'll get to it as fast as i can. 

## what happens next

i'll acknowledge the report, fix it in a private branch, and then push the patch. i'll make sure to give you credit in the changelog and the repo itself. 

thanks for keeping jhara safe for everyone.
